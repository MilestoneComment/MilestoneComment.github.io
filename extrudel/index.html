How it works

From image to STL
in five steps.

1

Drop your image

Drag a PNG, JPG, or JPEG onto the window — or click Browse. Black & white works best; Extrudel will threshold any image automatically.

2

Choose a scale mode

Circle for round objects, Rectangle for flat panels. Enter your target dimensions in millimetres.

3

Set depth and threshold

Depth controls how tall the extruded pixels stand. Threshold controls which pixels become solid — adjust until the preview looks right.

4

Add a base (optional)

Enable a base disc or base plate and set its thickness. 0.8 mm (two layers) gives a solid backing without adding much weight.

5

Convert and open in your slicer

Hit Convert to STL, choose a save location, and drop the file into Bambu Studio, PrusaSlicer, Cura, or any other slicer.
